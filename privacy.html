Privacy Policy

Hi there and welcome to our Privacy Policy which helps you to identify the type of relationship you may enter into with us in regard to your personal data.

In order to provide you with the best Service and experience exploring our Service, it is of vital importance that you fully understand this Privacy Policy.

This is the General Frequency Radio Privacy Policy ("Frequency", "We", "Us", "Our") and it applies to the application (the "app").

1. Private data we receive and collect

Personal data (or data) is any information relating to you and that alone or in combination with other information allows a person that collects and processes such information to identify you as an individual. It can be your name, address, your location data, or information related to your physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity. Personal data under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) also includes such technical information as a Media Access Control address (MAC-addresses), International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI), Unique Device Identifier (UDID), the Identity for Advertisers (IDFA), Internet Protocol address (IP-address), browser and system information.

1.1. Anonymous User

You are generally free to us our app anonymously. However, in such case, we still collect some of your personal data (Configuration data) when you voluntarily provide it to us or with the use of cookies and other similar technologies. In particular, if you are unauthorized, we collect data about the language and the latest station which you're listening to.

1.2. Account Data

When you login on our site, basic contact details are collected: your e-mail address and name. When you log in through a third-party account such as Google, Facebook, Twitter, Yahoo, Apple or similar services, you give us consent to extract from such an account your name, photo, email address, language preferences or other information which is clearly indicated when signing in with their help. Please pay attention to the description when you consent to such logging in.

1.3. Data collected in audio interchange with users

Certain partners of place third-parties cookies, web beacons and similar technologies on your machine for interchange with users. The data collected in such a way could include:

  • 1.Configuration data, including the information about the ads that were played to you in the last 24 months;
  • 2. Aggregate Ad specific information: number of impressions, number of clicks, numbers of users exposed, percentage of ad content played, reaction to the ad;
  • 3. Real-time general location data, such as town or zip code (but not the specific address);
  • 4. Technical information: browser identifiers; IDFA (Apple Identifier) or GAID (Google Advertising ID) for mobile app users (if enabled); IP Address; operating system and its version; device type; Device language;

We collect and process the personal data in order to provide you with all necessary services within our Site (including to make steps to conclude the contract with you). In particular, we collect and process different types of data for the following purposes:

  • 1. Account data: to provide you with our primary services, create and maintain your account, communicate with you at your request, to identify you as an App User;

  • 3. Data collected in interchange with users: to enable displaying audio-visual advertisements on the Site by means of the partner's software, to personalize your experience (better respond to your individual needs), as well as to fit the adverts and content that you see during and after using the app to your interests.

  • 1. Transparency: We will at all times keep you informed about changes to the processes to protect data privacy and security, including practices and policies. You may at any time request information on where and how data is stored, secured and used.
